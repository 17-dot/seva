The Sankat Saathi Seva Foundation

The Sankat Saathi Seva Foundation is a non-profit organisation headquartered in Bihar, India. The Foundation strives to eliminate classroom hunger by implementing the PM POSHAN (Mid-Day Meal) Programme.

It provides nutritious meals to children studying in government schools and government-aided schools. Akshaya Patra also aims to counter malnutrition and support the Right to Education of children hailing from socio-economically challenging backgrounds.

Our Vision

Gungun Aspires to Become an English Teacher

Gungun Kashyap is one of the 2.2 million beneficiaries of Akshaya Patra's mid-day meal programme and wishes to become an English Teacher.

With the support of nutritious meals provided by Akshaya Patra, Gungun has been able to focus on her studies and pursue her dreams of spreading knowledge through education.

Poetry and Purpose: Gayatri's Aspiring Future

Akshaya Patra supports Gayatri from Hampi with daily meals, helping her stay focused on studies and dream of becoming a bank manager and poet.

The nutritious meals have not only satisfied her hunger but also fueled her creativity and academic aspirations, proving that proper nutrition is key to unlocking potential.
