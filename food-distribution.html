About Food Distribution

Our Food Distribution program encompasses mid-day meals in schools, support for Anganwadi centers, breakfast programs, and assistance for homeless mothers. We focus on providing balanced, hygienic meals to ensure proper nutrition for growth and development.

Our Goal

To eliminate hunger as a barrier to health and education by serving 3 million nutritious meals daily by 2025.

Our Approach

Utilizing centralized kitchens with strict quality controls and efficient distribution networks to reach those in need.
