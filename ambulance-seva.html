About Ambulance Seva

Our Ambulance Seva initiative operates a fleet of well-equipped ambulances that provide free emergency medical transportation in rural and urban underserved areas. We partner with local hospitals and medical professionals to ensure rapid response times and quality care during transit.

Our Goal

To reduce mortality rates by providing immediate medical transportation to those in need, regardless of their economic background.

Our Approach

Deploying 24/7 ambulance services with trained paramedics and advanced life support equipment.
