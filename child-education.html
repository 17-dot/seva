About Child Education

Our Child Education initiative goes beyond meals to include educational resources, tutoring programs, and scholarships. We aim to ensure that every child has access to quality education without hunger or financial barriers.

Our Goal

To empower 3 million children with education and nutrition for a brighter future.

Our Approach

Integrating nutrition with educational support through partnerships with schools and communities.
