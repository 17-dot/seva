What We Do

Discover our comprehensive programs designed to eliminate classroom hunger and support education across India.

Our Primary Cause

Addressing the fundamental needs that enable children to learn and thrive

Nutritious Food

Providing balanced, wholesome meals that meet children's nutritional requirements for healthy growth and development.

Education Support

Eliminating hunger as a barrier to education, ensuring children can focus on learning and achieve their full potential.

Sustainability

Implementing environmentally responsible practices and creating long-term solutions for food security and education.

Community Impact

Building stronger communities by supporting children's health, education, and overall well-being through comprehensive programs.

How We Work to Make a Difference

Our comprehensive programs address various aspects of nutrition and education

PM POSHAN Programme

Our flagship mid-day meal program serves nutritious meals to school children across India. This government partnership ensures that children receive balanced nutrition to support their physical and cognitive development while encouraging school attendance.

2.25M+ Children | 23,111+ Schools

Homeless Mother Support

Supporting homeless mothers with nutritious meals and care during their most vulnerable times. This program addresses the critical nutritional needs of mothers and their children, helping to break the cycle of malnutrition and poverty.

Compassionate Care | Community Support

Anganwadi Feeding

Providing nutritious meals to children in Anganwadi centers to support early childhood development. This program focuses on the critical first years of life, ensuring proper nutrition during the foundational developmental period.

Early Childhood | Development Focus

Breakfast Program

Ensuring children start their day with nutritious breakfast to enhance learning capacity and school attendance. A healthy breakfast provides the energy needed for effective learning and concentration throughout the school day.

Morning Nutrition | Better Attendance

Our Comprehensive Approach

How we ensure maximum impact and sustainability

Centralized Kitchens

State-of-the-art kitchens with ISO certification ensure food safety and quality while maintaining efficient large-scale operations.

Quality Assurance

Rigorous testing and quality control processes ensure that every meal meets the highest nutritional and safety standards.

Efficient Distribution

Advanced logistics and transportation systems ensure timely delivery of fresh, hot meals to schools across multiple states.
